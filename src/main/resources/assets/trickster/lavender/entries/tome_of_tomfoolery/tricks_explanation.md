Trick types:

- Delusion: no-input value-generating tricks
- Stratagem: pure tricks operating on many inputs at once
- Distortion: fixed-input pure tricks
- Ploy: input-only and/or mana-consuming tricks
- Ambush: event types
- Revision: scribing patterns
